Q: What is the Swift-Campus platform?

A: Swift-Campus is a complaint management platform developed by students for managing campus-related complaints efficiently.

Q: Who can file a complaint?

A: Currently, any registered student can log in and file complaints. Staff members can view and resolve them.

Q: How do I get notified about complaint updates?

A: After filing a complaint, you will receive a confirmation email. You’ll also be notified when your complaint is resolved.
