Swift-Campus is a project developed by students at the Knight Foundation School of Computing and Information Science as part of the CEN 5011 – Advanced Software Engineering course under Dr. Charlyne Walker.

The goal of this application is to streamline the complaint submission process and ensure proper verification and resolution workflow, emphasizing usability and accountability.

We believe in clear communication, timely resolution, and user satisfaction. This project reflects our team's efforts in software verification, requirement adherence, and functional delivery.
